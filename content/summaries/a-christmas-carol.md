## Overview
Ebenezer Scrooge is wealthy, lonely, and proud of his harsh practicality. He dismisses kindness as weakness—until Christmas Eve brings visitors he can’t ignore.

## What happens (spoiler-light)
Scrooge is guided through vivid scenes from his life and shown the human cost of his choices. These visions force him to face what he lost, what he’s doing now, and where his path leads if he refuses to change.

## Key themes
- Compassion vs. selfishness  
- Regret, responsibility, and second chances  
- Poverty, inequality, and social duty  
- Transformation through honest self-evaluation  

## Who should read it
Ideal if you want a fast, emotionally satisfying classic with a big heart and strong message.
