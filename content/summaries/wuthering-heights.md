## Wuthering Heights Plot Summary
On the isolated Yorkshire moors, two households become entangled in a relationship defined by intensity, pride, and emotional violence. Heathcliff, an outsider brought into the family, forms a bond with Catherine that shapes every life around them.

## Wuthering Heights Plot Summary
When social ambition and wounded pride drive Catherine and Heathcliff apart, their choices trigger years of bitterness. The story unfolds through layered narration, showing how unresolved obsession can outlive the people who started it.

## Key Themes in Wuthering Heights
- Obsession vs. love  
- Revenge and generational harm  
- Class and belonging  
- Identity shaped by rejection  

## Why Wuthering Heights Still Matters
If you want a classic that’s stormy, psychological, and morally intense (not cozy romance), this is the one.
