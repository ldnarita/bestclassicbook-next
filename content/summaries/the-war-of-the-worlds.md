# The War of the Worlds Summary (Plot, Themes & Analysis)

**H. G. Wells · 6 min read · 1898**

A groundbreaking alien invasion story that questions human dominance.

---

## The War of the Worlds Plot Summary
Martians invade Earth with advanced technology, overwhelming humanity in days. Society collapses as survival becomes the only goal.

---

## The War of the Worlds Plot Summary

As cities fall, the narrator struggles to survive in a world suddenly made fragile. The novel challenges the belief that humans rule the planet unquestioned.

---

## Key Themes in The War of the Worlds
- Imperialism reversed  
- Human vulnerability  
- Survival and panic  
- Technology vs. biology  
- Civilization’s fragility

---
