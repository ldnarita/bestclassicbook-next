# Dracula Summary (Plot, Themes & Analysis)

**Bram Stoker · 7 min read · 1897**

A chilling tale of obsession, fear, and the battle between modern reason and ancient evil.

---

## Dracula Plot Summary
Count Dracula leaves Transylvania for England, bringing darkness with him. Told through letters and journal entries, the novel follows a group of unlikely heroes as they uncover and confront a supernatural threat.

---

## Dracula Plot Summary

Jonathan Harker encounters Dracula in his castle and barely escapes. Soon after, strange illnesses and deaths follow the Count to England. Professor Van Helsing leads the effort to understand the vampire’s powers and stop him before he spreads further.

---

## Key Themes in Dracula
- Fear of the unknown  
- Science vs. superstition  
- Power, control, and seduction  
- Immigration anxiety and cultural change  
- Good vs. evil

---

## Why Dracula Still Matters
