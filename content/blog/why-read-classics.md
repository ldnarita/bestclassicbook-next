## Classics Expand Perspective

Classic books allow us to see the world through different eras and social realities.

## They Improve Thinking

Dense language and layered themes improve critical thinking and vocabulary.

## Cultural Literacy

Many modern stories reference classics. Reading them deepens understanding.

## They Stand the Test of Time

If a book is still read after 200 years, it contains something powerful.

Classic books can feel intimidating. The trick is picking the right entry point.

## Start with the right category
- Plot-driven
- Short
- Modern prose feel
- High cultural payoff
